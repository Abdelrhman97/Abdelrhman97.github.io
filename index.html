Information About project

The BMS enhances battery performance in the best possible way, extends its lifespan, and prevents hazardous conditions. The system also records all operations and errors that may occur during battery use, facilitating maintenance procedures and clear details about battery performance and its condition can be accessed, allowing necessary actions to be taken in case of any faults or damages.
